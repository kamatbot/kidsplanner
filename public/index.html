🎓 Add your school's calendar

Paste any public calendar link your school (or a club) gives you — we'll check it works before saving.

Where do I find this link?

  1. Open Google Calendar on the web.
  2. Hover the school's calendar in the left sidebar → click the ⋮ (Options) → Settings and sharing.
  3. Scroll to Integrate calendar.
  4. Copy the Public address in iCal format (or the Secret address in iCal format if the calendar isn't public) and paste it above.
  5. A school "Add to Google Calendar" link works too — open it, then follow the steps above.
  1. Go to your school portal's Calendar page.
  2. Click Export calendar.
  3. Choose All events and a date range.
  4. Click Get calendar URL and copy the iCal link above.
  1. Find the calendar's Publish or Subscribe link (Apple/iCloud) or the shared calendar's ICS link (Outlook / Office 365).
  2. Copy the link — it may start with webcal://, that's fine, we'll convert it automatically.

Paste any .ics or webcal:// link your school gives you — most calendar systems (Google, Moodle, Blackbaud, ManageBac, club sign-up sites) can generate one. If a link doesn't work, it's usually a private web page rather than a calendar feed — look for wording like "iCal", "ICS", "subscribe", or "export calendar".

Feed stopped working? Just re-paste the URL above — schools sometimes reset a "secret" calendar address.
