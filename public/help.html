How do I sign in without a password?

Fam ETC uses passkeys (Face ID, Touch ID, or your device PIN) instead of passwords. If you lose access to your device, use a backup code from the sign-in page.

How do I add my kids?

Once you're signed in, go to Settings → Manage Family and add each kid's name, grade, and a color. Kids don't sign up or log in themselves.
