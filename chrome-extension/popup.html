Fam ETC School Import

Auto-syncs while you're signed in — this is the manual fallback. Pulls homework & timetable from your logged-in Moodle session into Fam ETC.

Requires both a Moodle tab (logged in) and a fametc.com tab (logged in) open. Auto-sync runs automatically on Moodle pages every ~10 minutes once your kids' Moodle IDs are set in Fam ETC → Settings.
